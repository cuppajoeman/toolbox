Put this in your debug profile: 
  
[conf]
tools.build:cflags=["-fsanitize=address"]
tools.build:cxxflags=["-fsanitize=address"]
tools.build:exelinkflags=["-fsanitize=address"]
tools.build:sharedlinkflags=["-fsanitize=address"]

If it still doesn't work try a conan install . --profile=debug --build=*, you can also try removing your build dir.
